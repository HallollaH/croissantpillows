So how'd it all start?

Waffle McIron was sitting on his couch one day, and was extremely hungry, but also extremely lazy. He understood how much effort it would take to get food, so instead he spent days creating the Croissant Pillow, and now, it's ready for the world to see. The Science behind it

The Invention of it.

The Croissant Pillow was invented when Waffle McIron found a way to make croissants at 200x their usual size. Using a rapidly expanding solution, the croissant can grow up to 4 miles wide, making it perfect for militaries. McIron immediatly filed a patent for the technology. The extremely advanced technology allows us to repair your pillow remotely.

Development

Over a 7 year period, the technology was refined to the point where one pillow could be made in less than 35 seconds.
